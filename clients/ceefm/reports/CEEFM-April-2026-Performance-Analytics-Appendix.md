### Top 5 conversion findings

## PageSpeed Insights (Pre-Patch Baseline)

Pre-patch PageSpeed measurements taken 2026-04-30 morning, against the live deploy at the time:

| Surface | Performance | FCP | LCP | TBT | CLS | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Desktop | 94 / 100 | 0.9s | 1.2s | Good | 0.01 | Green |
| Mobile | 76 / 100 | 2.7s | 5.0s | 100ms | 0.01 | LCP Poor |

Desktop landed cleanly in the green band. Mobile flagged Largest Contentful Paint at 5.0s, which Google's threshold treats as Poor (target under 2.5s). Root cause: the font cascade was loading 24+ woff2 files including unused Cyrillic and Vietnamese subsets, blocking the critical render path for ~1,800ms.

### Performance patch deployed 2026-04-30 evening

- Font subsets reduced to latin and latin-ext only (24+ woff2 files down to 12), eliminating Cyrillic and Vietnamese subsets that were never used by the EN/HU site
- Hero stats counter converted from a hydrated React island to static Astro markup
- Cookie banner converted from a hydrated React island to static HTML with a small inline script
- Contact form hydration switched from `client:load` to `client:visible` (delays JS until the form is scrolled into view)
- Unsplash images reduced from `q=80&w=800` to `q=60&w=640` and given a `preconnect` resource hint instead of `dns-prefetch`

### Expected post-patch mobile (estimated, re-test queued)

- Performance: 86-90 (target 90+)
- FCP: 1.6 to 2.0s
- LCP: 3.4 to 3.8s (still flags as Needs Improvement; target green is 2.5s)

Hitting full green on LCP requires inlining critical CSS for Faq.css and Footer.css plus self-hosting fonts with explicit `font-display: swap` (already shipped via @fontsource v5 default). That work is queued for Wk 7.

Accessibility currently sits at 88 mobile and 84 desktop. Both are correctable in Wk 7-8 alongside the service page builds (alt text completeness, heading hierarchy, form label improvements).
