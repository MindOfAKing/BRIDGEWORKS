# CEEFM April 2026 Report Package Structure

The current monthly report is strong but too long as a single client-facing document. Split it into a concise main report plus focused appendices.

## Recommended Package

### 1. Executive Report

Target length: 6-8 pages.

Audience: Victor / decision-maker.

Purpose: Tell the story, make the case, ask for decisions.

Include:
- At a Glance
- Month in Summary
- Headline metrics only
- What changed in April
- What is pending
- May Plan A / Plan B summary
- Decisions needed at the May call
- Closing note

Exclude:
- Long delivery inventories
- Full analytics tables
- Long technical explanations
- All raw evidence

### 2. Delivery Appendix

Target length: 8-12 pages.

Audience: Victor, internal BridgeWorks, accountability review.

Purpose: Prove what shipped.

Include:
- Proposal commitments closed
- Shipped 2026-04-30
- Platform foundation items
- Google Ads readiness assets
- Cancellations and truth corrections
- Engagement progress table
- File/source references

### 3. Performance and Analytics Appendix

Target length: 8-12 pages.

Audience: Victor, marketing review, future comparison.

Purpose: Preserve measurement detail without overloading the executive report.

Include:
- GEO score progression
- Marketing audit breakdown
- LinkedIn analytics charts and commentary
- PageSpeed screenshots/metrics
- Rich Results evidence
- Bing/GBP/Wikidata status
- Retest queue

### 4. May Action Plan

Target length: 4-6 pages.

Audience: Victor and BridgeWorks delivery team.

Purpose: Turn the report into decisions and execution.

Include:
- Plan A: SolaCare absorbed
- Plan B: SolaCare separate
- Side-by-side comparison
- Week 6-9 execution plan
- KPI targets
- Budget questions
- Required Victor inputs

## Best Client Delivery Format

Send one PDF pack with this order:

1. Executive Report
2. May Action Plan
3. Delivery Appendix
4. Performance and Analytics Appendix

If sending by email, attach only the Executive Report and May Action Plan. Keep the appendices available as "supporting evidence on request" or send them separately after the call.

## Recommended Naming

- `CEEFM-April-2026-Executive-Report.pdf`
- `CEEFM-May-2026-Action-Plan.pdf`
- `CEEFM-April-2026-Delivery-Appendix.pdf`
- `CEEFM-April-2026-Performance-Analytics-Appendix.pdf`
