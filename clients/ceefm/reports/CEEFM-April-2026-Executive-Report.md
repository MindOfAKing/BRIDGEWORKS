## Month in Summary

April 2026 closed Month 1 of the 16-week engagement. The headline result is structural. CEEFM's website moved from a 16/100 GEO baseline (Critical band, AI systems could not reliably cite or recommend the brand) to 74/100 (top of the Fair band, one point below Good). The site now has full AI crawler access, a complete ProfessionalService entity, security headers, Hungarian legal imprint, zero false claims in schema, a Wikidata Q-item published as ground truth for entity recognition (Q139592822), Bing verified with sitemap submitted, IndexNow integration live, Google Business Profile claimed and in postcard verification, LinkedIn fully optimised, and Rich Results passing for the Local Business entity. This is the foundation every subsequent stage of the engagement runs on.

The month's principal decision was the Week 5 re-sequence. A mid-April structural audit revealed that paid acquisition launched on the foundation as it stood would have inflated cost per lead, surfaced credibility gaps to every prospect who clicked through, and risked triggering a Google manual action on a self-declared rating without review nodes. Phase 3 ad and outreach work were jointly deferred to Phase 4 (Wk 9-12). Wk 5-8 became the foundation sprint.

April delivered fully on content (12 of 12 posts, calendar shipped on time), fully on website completion (standalone /contact pages live, performance patch deployed), and zero on paid acquisition by intent (deferred). The retainer was deployed in full, redirected from ad management and cold outreach into the GEO foundation track. That redirection is what produced the +58 point GEO lift in 30 days, of which +27 points landed on 2026-04-30 alone via the Wikidata, Bing, IndexNow, Google Business Profile, LinkedIn, and PageSpeed work.

The month also closed on three cancellations: brand visual package (227,300 HUF declined), solar add-on (200,024 HUF declined), and the false EU Ecolabel claim removed from schema. One productive surprise: Victor's own Claude-generated growth analysis, which has been read, mapped against contracted scope, and integrated into the May plan.
