## Why no URL or phone in the description

GBP's business description field is for telling potential customers what the business does. URLs and phone numbers are forbidden here because GBP provides dedicated fields and prominent buttons on the listing for those:

- Website button (set in the Website field, links to ceefm.eu)
- Call button (set in the Phone field, dials +36 30 600 5400)
- Directions button (uses the address)

Cramming URLs into the description is what triggered the "invalid value" rejection.

## Snippet optimization

Google often shows the first ~150 characters of the description in search results. Both versions lead with the strongest signals (location, service, geography) so the snippet reads:

> "CEEFM Kft is a Budapest-based facility management company serving hotels, aparthotels, residential complexes, and student housing across Hungary."

That is the single most important sentence. Everything after is supporting detail.
