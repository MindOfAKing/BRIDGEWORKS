## How to apply

Google Ads RSA scoring rewards diversity, completeness, and length of components. The fastest path from Weak to Good is to fill all 15 headline slots and all 4 description slots with distinct angles, then unpin most fields so the ML can rotate freely.

1. In Google Ads, open the ad group → ad → Edit
2. Paste the 15 headlines below into the headline slots (one per slot)
3. Paste the 4 descriptions into the description slots
4. **Pinning rules**:
   - Optionally pin Headline 1 (Premium FM in Budapest) to Position 1 to guarantee a category-cue first impression
   - Do NOT pin more than 2 headlines or 1 description; over-pinning is the most common Ad Strength penalty
5. Save and check the Ad Strength meter; should move to Good or Excellent

If the Ad Strength still reads Weak after applying all 15 + 4, the issue is usually keyword alignment. Make sure at least 3 of the headlines contain the ad group's primary keyword (for the boutique-hospitality group, that's `facility management Budapest` or `aparthotel takarítás`).

## Note on Ad Strength meter

If "Weak" persists after the full 15 + 4 are loaded:

- Check no headline is duplicated word-for-word in another headline (Google rejects near-duplicates)
- Check that at least 1 keyword from the ad group's keyword list appears in at least 3 headlines
- Reduce pinning to 0 or 1 fields total
- Ensure Final URL matches the ad group theme (don't point hospitality ads to a residential page)
