## 1. Banner Image Brief

**Dimensions:** 1128 × 191 px (LinkedIn company page banner spec)
**Tool:** Canva (free). Use the "LinkedIn Company Banner" template.

**Composition:**
- Background: deep CEEFM green gradient (#1C3D2A to #2D5942) or dark navy if the green clashes with the page logo on small screens. Test both.
- Top-left text block: brand tagline in serif type, white. "Premium Facility Management for Property and Hospitality."
- Bottom-right small text: "Trusted by Limehome · 9.4 cleanliness score · 24 months above 9.0."
- Subtle motif: thin gold horizontal rule (~2px) under the tagline. Matches site palette.
- No logo on the banner. LinkedIn already shows the page logo overlaid on top-left of the banner.

**Do not use Unsplash stock.** Solid colour gradient + type only is cleaner and consistent with the on-site identity.

If a banner is not produced this week, leave LinkedIn's default empty banner rather than upload a placeholder. A blank banner reads as understated, a wrong banner reads as careless.

## 10. What to Track After Apply

Per the proposal, monthly performance review covers LinkedIn alongside ads/SEO. The metrics that matter for a B2B FM company page:

- Page followers (baseline today: 146; target +50 by 2026-05-30)
- Page views per week
- Unique visitors per week
- Search appearances (LinkedIn analytics shows the queries the page surfaced for)
- Engagement rate per post (target 3% of impressions)
- Custom button clicks (this is the closest LinkedIn analog to a conversion)

These all live in the native LinkedIn analytics dashboard (Admin view → Analytics).
