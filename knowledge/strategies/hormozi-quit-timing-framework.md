## What it is
A framework for deciding when quitting a business, project, or strategy is the right move versus persisting through difficulty. Hormozi distinguishes between quitting due to fear and quitting due to logic. No transcript available; insight drawn from title and Acquisition.com framing.

## How it works
1. Identify whether the desire to quit comes from emotion (pain, fear, comparison) or data (model is broken, market is wrong)
2. If emotional: do not quit; change tactics instead
3. If data-driven: set a specific decision date and metrics threshold before evaluating
4. Ask: "Is my path to the outcome blocked, or is it just hard?"
5. Quit the strategy, not the goal
