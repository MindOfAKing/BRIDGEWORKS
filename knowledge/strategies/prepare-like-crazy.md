## What it is
Hormozi's argument is that most people underestimate the ratio of preparation to performance required at a high level. The difference between good and great in sales, client calls, content, and delivery is almost entirely in the preparation done before the moment itself.

## How it works
- Before any important call, pitch, or presentation, over-research the other party.
- Prepare three times as many talking points as you expect to use.
- Anticipate objections and prepare full responses in advance.
- Practice the opening and the close until they are automatic.
- Enter high-stakes moments with no open questions about your own material.

## BridgeWorks application
For BridgeWorks, this applies directly to client discovery calls and proposal presentations. Over-preparing on a prospect's industry, existing tools, and pain points before a call signals expertise and builds trust faster -- especially with Nigerian SME founders who are skeptical of agencies. For Emmanuel's personal brand, it means drafting and rehearsing LinkedIn content before publishing, not improvising the message.
